What is The Beacon of Hope?


The Beacon of Hope is a TRANScend Together charity community, a dedicated collection of individuals dedicated to our purpose of [purpose]. We share a vision of a future in which all LGBTQ+ youth feel loved, seen, and celebrated, and The Beacon of Hope illuminates the path to that goal. Some Beacon of Hope are recognized as members of the following organizations:

Teen Trans-Care

This is a charity made by Frederick Ram who would like to support transgender teens and get the medical care that they need

Support for Wildfires

This is a charity made by Joseph Giovani that supports the animals who's homes were destroyed in the recent wildfire.

Homeless Queer Teens

This is a charity made by Chesca Arqabioso to support teens who have been thrown out of their homes due to their identities.

Abandoned Pets

This is a charity made by Lunala Astro to help support fosters who adopt animals that were abandoned on the road by their owners.

Acceptance Groups

This is a charity made by Jorel Mendoza to both create and fund certain queer groups in schools where students are free to express themselves without the threat of being harmed for it.
