Support for Wildfires

$167 Raised

Goal: $2,000

Where the flames end, we will be found.

This is a charity made by Joseph Giovani due to the wildfires misplacing animals from their habitats and causing endless destruction.


As a man who has worked as a Wildfire Lookout, he knew the dangers and risks of both the before of the wildfire and its aftermath effects on the environment and how it impacts both humans and animals.


This charity is a way to show our love and support for both animals and people in need and show that our community will always have each other's backs.


Thank you, to all those that donate.
