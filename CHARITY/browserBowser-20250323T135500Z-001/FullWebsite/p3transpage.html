Homeless Queer Teens

$220 Raised

Goal: $4,000

We will be the home you need.

This is a charity made by Franchesca Argabioso for providing shelter and support for teens who have been thrown out of their homes due to coming out or being outed by peers.


As someone who accepts people regardless of gender, she knows the safety and happiness of a child far more outweighs anything else. Therefore, she decided that the cases of homelessness is rising due to the cruelty of certain parents and wishes to help them as much as she can.


We hope that this charity will make others feel seen and cared for especially in these hard times.


Thank you, to all those that donate.
