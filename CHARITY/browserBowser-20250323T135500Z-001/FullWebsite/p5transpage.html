Acceptance Groups

$1,735 Raised

Goal: $2,000

Together, for all those in need.

This is a charity made by Jorel Mendoza in an attempt to create more support groups for queer students in schools and communities.


Being a student I knew I couldn't do much else other than try to convince the school to create queer-friendly spaces for his classmates and this is an attempt in a movement for not just his school but also other schools to make safer environments for their queer students.


This charity is a way to show our love and support for those teenagers and hopefully get schools to further more support their students in their self-expression and identity.


Thank you, to all those that donate!
