Together, for all those in need.

This is a charity made by Freferick Ram who would like to support transgender teens and get the medical care that they need.


As a transgender teen themselves, they know the value of being able to start as early as possible when it comes to hormone blockers and the other hormonal treatments needed, as this sets them up for an easier time in adulthood.


This charity is a way to show our love and support for both animals and people in need and show that our community will always have each other's backs.


Thank you, to all those that donate!
