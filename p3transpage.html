Homeless Queer Teens

We will be the home you need.

Story

This is a charity made by Chesca Arqabioso for providing shelter and support for teens who have been thrown out of their homes due to coming out or being outed by peers.

Being a mother herself, she knows that the safety and happiness of a child far more outweighs anything else when you are a parent, therefore she decided that the cases of homelessness is rising due to the cruelty of certain parents and wishes to help them as much as she can.

We hope this charity will make others feel seen and cared for especially in these hard times.

Thank you, to all those that donate!
