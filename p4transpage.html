Story

This is a charity made by Lunala Astro regarding the numerous amounts of stray animals found on the street abandoned by their owners.

Pets should never be abandoned, sometimes circumstances call for measures like that but there's a reason we have developed shelters for these
animals to go to and find a better chance at having a home. Abandoning them in the streets makes it dangerous for both them and the neighborhood around, putting all at risk.

This charity was made to show awareness and hopefully convince others to take action whend it comes to these concerns about both animals and our community.

Thank you, to all those that donate!
